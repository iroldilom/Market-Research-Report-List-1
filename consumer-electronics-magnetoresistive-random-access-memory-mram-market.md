<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Consumer Electronics Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) market primarily consists of two types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes magnetic fields to switch the memory state, offering high speed but with limited scalability. In contrast, STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM) employs a spin-polarized current to achieve data writing, allowing for higher densities and endurance. Both types are valued for their non-volatility, low power consumption, and potential to replace traditional memory technologies in consumer devices.</p></p>
