<p><p>A Balloon Dilation Catheter is a specialized medical device used primarily to treat obstructed blood vessels and certain structural heart conditions. This minimally invasive procedure is commonly utilized in percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA) and other vascular interventions.</p><p>The Balloon Dilation Catheter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.70% during the forecast period.
