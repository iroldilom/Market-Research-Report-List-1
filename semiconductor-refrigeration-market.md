<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor refrigeration market comprises two main types: single-stage and multi-stage refrigeration systems. Single-stage systems utilize a single thermoelectric module to achieve cooling for applications requiring moderate temperature differences, making them cost-effective and simpler. In contrast, multi-stage systems incorporate multiple thermoelectric modules stacked together, allowing for greater temperature differentials and more efficient cooling for demanding applications like aerospace and scientific instruments. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, balancing performance, complexity, and cost.</p></p>
