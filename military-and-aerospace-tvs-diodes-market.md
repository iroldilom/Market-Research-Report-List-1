<p><p>The Military and Aerospace TVS Diodes market is segmented into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar TVS diodes protect circuits from voltage spikes by allowing current to flow in one direction, making them ideal for positive voltage applications. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can handle voltage surges in both directions, offering enhanced protection for applications with alternating current or complex signal environments. These components are crucial for safeguarding sensitive electronics in demanding military and aerospace applications against transient voltage threats.</p></p>
