<p><p>Emergency Shutdown Valves (ESVs) are critical in safety management across various industries, categorized by their actuation methods: electrical, hydraulic, and pneumatic. </p><p>Electrical ESVs use electrical signals for actuation, offering quick response times and integration with automated systems. Hydraulic ESVs rely on fluid pressure, ensuring powerful force and control, ideal for high-pressure applications. Pneumatic ESVs utilize compressed air for actuation, providing rapid operation and simplicity in design. Each type serves specific operational needs and safety requirements, enhancing overall system reliability.</p></p>
