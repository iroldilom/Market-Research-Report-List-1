<p><p>The intubation scopes market encompasses two primary types: rigid and flexible intubation scopes. Rigid intubation scopes are characterized by their solid construction, providing stability and a direct line of sight during intubation, often used in emergency and surgical settings. In contrast, flexible intubation scopes feature a malleable design that allows for navigation through complex airways, making them suitable for challenging intubation cases. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing patient safety and facilitating effective airway management.</p></p>
