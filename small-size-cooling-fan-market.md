<p><p>The small size cooling fan market is categorized into three main types: axial fans, centrifugal fans, and others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis, making them efficient for cooling applications requiring high airflow at low pressures. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air in parallel and discharge it at right angles, providing higher pressure and flow rates, suitable for ducted systems. "Others" may include specialized fan designs tailored for specific applications, such as electronics or household uses.</p></p>
