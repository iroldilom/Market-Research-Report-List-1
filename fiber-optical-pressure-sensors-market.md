<p><p>Fiber optical pressure sensors are classified into two main types: digital and analog. Digital type sensors convert pressure measurements into digital signals, offering precise and easily interpretable data, which is ideal for advanced monitoring systems. Conversely, analog type sensors produce continuous voltage or current signals proportional to pressure, allowing for straightforward integration into existing analog systems. Both types cater to various applications, including industrial monitoring, medical devices, and environmental assessments, with selections based on desired sensitivity, accuracy, and system compatibility.</p></p>
