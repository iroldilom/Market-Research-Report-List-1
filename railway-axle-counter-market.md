<p><strong>The Railway Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Axle Counter</li><li>On-rail Axle Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Railway Axle Counter market consists of two primary types: Rail Side Axle Counters and On-Rail Axle Counters. Rail Side Axle Counters are installed alongside the tracks and detect train passages by sensing the axles as they pass. On-Rail Axle Counters, on the other hand, are mounted directly on the train's axles, providing real-time data by measuring the distance traveled. Both types are essential for enhancing rail safety, monitoring traffic flow, and ensuring efficient train operations.</p></p>
