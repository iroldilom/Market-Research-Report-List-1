<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) tyre market primarily consists of two types: radial tyres and bias tyres. Radial tyres, characterized by their perpendicular cord layers, offer improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and enhanced durability, making them popular for electric and hybrid vehicles. Conversely, bias tyres feature crisscrossed layers, providing a softer ride but generally lower efficiency and performance. As the NEV market grows, radial tyres are increasingly favored for their performance benefits, while bias tyres may be used in specific applications.</p></p>
