<p><p>The stainless steel market comprises various types, including ferritic, austenitic, martensitic, PH (precipitation hardened), and duplex stainless steel. Ferritic stainless steel is known for its good corrosion resistance and magnetic properties. Austenitic stainless steel offers excellent toughness and formability. Martensitic stainless steel is characterized by high strength and hardness. PH stainless steel combines high strength with corrosion resistance, while duplex stainless steel blends properties of both austenitic and ferritic types, providing enhanced strength and resistance. Other types include specialized alloys for specific applications.</p></p>
