<p><strong>The Thermal Electric Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal electric actuator market is primarily divided into two types: normally closed and normally open actuators. Normally closed types maintain the default position when de-energized, ensuring safety by blocking flow until activated. In contrast, normally open types allow flow when de-energized, shutting off when activated. Each type caters to specific applications, such as HVAC systems and industrial processes, where precise control of fluid flow or air movement is essential for efficiency and reliability in operation.</p></p>
