<p><p>The Cold Spray Equipment Market is primarily segmented into High Pressure Cold Spray (HPCS) and Low Pressure Cold Spray (LPCS) systems. HPCS utilizes high-velocity gas to propel solid powders onto surfaces, creating dense coatings, suitable for demanding applications in aerospace and defense. Conversely, LPCS operates at lower pressures, allowing for softer materials to be deposited, making it ideal for repairing and coating sensitive components. Each type caters to specific industry needs, influencing material properties and final applications.</p></p>
