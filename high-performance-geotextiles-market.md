<p><strong>The High Performance Geotextiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non Woven High Performance Geotextiles</li><li>Woven High Performance Geotextiles</li><li>Knitted High Performance Geotextiles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Performance Geotextiles market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Non-Woven High Performance Geotextiles** are created by bonding fibers through chemical, mechanical, or thermal processes, offering excellent filtration and drainage properties.</p><p>2. **Woven High Performance Geotextiles** are made by interlacing yarns for superior tensile strength and stability, commonly used in reinforcing applications.</p><p>3. **Knitted High Performance Geotextiles** are produced through a looping technique, resulting in flexibility and stretch, making them suitable for various applications in civil engineering and landscaping.</p></p>
