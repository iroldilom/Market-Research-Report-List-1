<p><p>The cationic light cured resin market comprises several key components: </p><p>1. **Oligomers** are the backbone of the resin, providing mechanical strength and flexibility.</p><p>2. **Monomers** are low molecular weight compounds that enhance reactivity and reduce viscosity, aiding application.</p><p>3. **Photoinitiators** trigger the curing process when exposed to light, promoting rapid hardening.</p><p>4. **Additives** enhance specific properties, such as adhesion, durability, and UV resistance, tailoring the resin for various applications in industries like coatings, adhesives, and dental materials.</p></p>
