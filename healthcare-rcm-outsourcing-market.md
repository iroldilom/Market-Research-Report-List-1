<p><strong>The Healthcare RCM Outsourcing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-intervention</li><li>Intervention</li><li>Post-intervention</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) outsourcing market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Pre-intervention**: Focuses on services such as patient registration, eligibility verification, and coding, ensuring accurate data capture before services are rendered.</p><p>2. **Intervention**: Involves active billing, claims submission, and payment posting, aiming to optimize revenue during patient encounters.</p><p>3. **Post-intervention**: Entails follow-up on unpaid claims, denial management, and reporting, ensuring financial recovery and analysis after services are completed. Each phase supports efficient revenue flow in the healthcare system.</p></p>
