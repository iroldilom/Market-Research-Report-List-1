<p><p>The Automated Print Quality Inspection System market is divided into two main types: in-line and off-line print quality inspection systems. In-line systems are integrated into the production process, enabling real-time monitoring and immediate corrections to maintain print quality without halting operations. Conversely, off-line systems analyze prints after production, allowing for detailed inspections in a controlled environment. Both systems enhance printing efficiency, reduce waste, and ensure high-quality outcomes, catering to the demands of various industries like packaging and labels.</p></p>
