Organic fining agents, including egg whites and casein, enhance mouthfeel and flavor while maintaining a natural profile. Complex fining agents, which combine both organic and inorganic components, offer versatile solutions for achieving clarity and stability in wines. Each type plays a crucial role in improving wine quality and consumer appeal.</p></p>
