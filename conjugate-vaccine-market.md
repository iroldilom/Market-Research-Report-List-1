<p><p>The conjugate vaccine market encompasses various types, including Hib, meningococcal, and pneumococcal vaccines. Pneumococcal vaccines safeguard against Streptococcus pneumoniae, reducing pneumonia and meningitis risk. The "Others" category includes vaccines for less common pathogens or combination vaccines. These vaccines are vital for public health, decreasing morbidity and mortality associated with bacterial infections across different age groups.</p></p>
