<p><p>Mirror replacement with cameras in commercial vehicles involves substituting traditional side mirrors with advanced camera systems. These systems typically utilize CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low light performance, ideal for nighttime driving. In contrast, CMOS cameras are more energy-efficient and offer faster processing speeds, making them suitable for real-time monitoring. Together, these technologies enhance visibility, reduce blind spots, and improve overall safety in commercial vehicle operations.</p></p>
