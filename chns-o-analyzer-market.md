<p><p>The CHNS (O) Analyzer market encompasses three main types: GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC Chromatography utilizes gas chromatography to separate and analyze compounds, providing accurate elemental composition data. Frontal Chromatography involves the continuous introduction of a sample to determine its composition based on retention times. Adsorption-Desorption techniques measure how substances interact with solid surfaces, enabling the analysis of complex mixtures by examining how different components adhere or detach under specific conditions. Each method offers unique advantages for elemental analysis.</p></p>
