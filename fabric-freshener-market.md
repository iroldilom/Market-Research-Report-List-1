<p><p>The fabric freshener market can be categorized primarily into solid and spray types. Spray fabric fresheners, on the other hand, deliver an immediate burst of fragrance and are typically used directly on fabrics to refresh clothing, upholstery, and linens. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for convenience and effectiveness in maintaining fabric freshness.</p></p>
