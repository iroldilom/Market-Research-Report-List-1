<p><ul><li>Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder)</li><li>ANFO</li><li>Emulsion Explosive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mining explosive market comprises various types, primarily including ammonium nitrate explosives, ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil), and emulsion explosives. Ammonium nitrate explosives, often in powder form, are essential for high-performance blasting operations. ANFO, a blend of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, is cost-effective and widely used for bulk blasting. Emulsion explosives, which utilize a water-resistant mixture, offer superior safety and performance in wet conditions. Together, these products support efficient mineral extraction and enhance operational productivity in mining.</p></p>
