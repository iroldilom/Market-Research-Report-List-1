<p><p>Laser Doppler Anemometers (LDA) are advanced measurement tools used to analyze fluid velocities by employing the Doppler effect of laser light scattered by particles within the flow. The market is segmented into two main types: Forward Scatter Receivers, which capture light scattered in the direction of the laser beam, offering high sensitivity and resolution, and Backward Scatter Receivers, which detect light scattered back toward the source, providing better performance in turbid or dense media. Both types serve diverse industrial and research applications.</p></p>
