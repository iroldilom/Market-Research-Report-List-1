<p><p>The epoxy topcoat market is categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based epoxy topcoats are known for their durability and resistance to harsh chemicals, making them suitable for industrial applications. However, they can have higher levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based epoxy topcoats are environmentally friendlier, offering easier application and cleanup, but may not match the performance of solvent-based options in extreme conditions. Both types cater to diverse customer needs across various sectors.</p></p>
