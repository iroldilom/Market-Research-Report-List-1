<p><strong>The Stationary Dehumidifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Dehumidifier</li><li>Air Duct Type Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary dehumidifier market features two main types: conventional dehumidifiers and air duct type dehumidifiers. Conventional dehumidifiers operate independently, using a refrigerant system to extract moisture from the air, making them suitable for smaller spaces like basements or laundry rooms. In contrast, air duct type dehumidifiers are integrated into existing HVAC systems, effectively managing humidity levels throughout larger buildings. Both types serve the essential function of maintaining optimal humidity, protecting interiors from mold and moisture-related damage.</p></p>
