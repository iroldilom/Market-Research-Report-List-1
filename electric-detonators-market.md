<p><p>The Electric Detonators market is primarily categorized into two types: Instantaneous Electric Detonators and Delay Electric Detonators. Instantaneous Electric Detonators are designed for immediate detonation upon receiving an electric signal, often used in scenarios requiring precision timing. In contrast, Delay Electric Detonators incorporate a built-in delay mechanism, allowing for timed detonations that can enhance safety and control during blasting operations. Both types serve essential roles in mining, construction, and demolition, ensuring effective and safe explosive applications.</p></p>
