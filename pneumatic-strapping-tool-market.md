<p><p>The pneumatic strapping tool market can be divided into automatic and semi-automatic types. Automatic pneumatic strapping tools streamline the strapping process by applying tension and sealing straps with minimal operator intervention, enhancing productivity and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume applications. On the other hand, semi-automatic pneumatic strapping tools require some manual input from the operator, offering flexibility and control while still benefiting from pneumatic power. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across industries, ensuring efficient strapping solutions.</p></p>
