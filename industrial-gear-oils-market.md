<p><p>Industrial gear oils are specialized lubricants designed to protect and enhance the performance of gears in industrial machinery. **Mineral industrial gear oils** are derived from crude oil, offering cost-effective lubrication for standard operating conditions. **Synthetic industrial gear oils** are chemically engineered for superior performance, excelling in extreme temperatures and heavy loads. **Worm gear oils** are formulated specifically for worm gear systems, providing high film strength and anti-wear properties to handle sliding motion and high friction. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal gear protection and efficiency.</p></p>
