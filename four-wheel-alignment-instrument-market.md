<p><strong>The Four Wheel Alignment Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Wheel Aligner</li><li>3D Wheel Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Four Wheel Alignment Instrument market consists primarily of two types: CCD Wheel Aligners and 3D Wheel Aligners. CCD Wheel Aligners utilize charge-coupled device (CCD) technology to measure angles and positions, providing accurate alignment diagnostics using reflectors. In contrast, 3D Wheel Aligners employ advanced optical technology to capture real-time data in three dimensions, offering enhanced precision and faster measurements. Both types are essential for maintaining vehicle performance, but 3D systems are increasingly favored for their superior accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
