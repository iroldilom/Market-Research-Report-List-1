<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Card Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-to-Card Printers</li><li>Retransfer Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The card printers market primarily consists of two types: Direct-to-Card Printers and Retransfer Printers. Direct-to-Card Printers apply dye directly onto the card surface, offering fast printing speeds and lower costs, making them ideal for low to mid-volume applications. In contrast, Retransfer Printers use a heat and pressure process to transfer an image onto a film layer before applying it to the card, resulting in higher print quality and durability, suitable for security-focused or high-volume demands.</p></p>
