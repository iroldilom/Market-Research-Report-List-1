<p><p>The Contact Signal Relay market is classified into two main types: Single Coil and Double Coil relays. Single Coil relays operate with a single electromagnetic coil, enabling simpler designs and lower costs, making them suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, Double Coil relays feature two coils that allow for latching functionality, providing enhanced reliability and reducing power consumption during operation. This makes Double Coil relays ideal for applications requiring stable states without continuous power, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
