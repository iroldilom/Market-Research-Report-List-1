<p><p>The semiconductor sealing market features various O-ring materials tailored for different applications. FFKM (perfluoroelastomer) offers exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it ideal for harsh environments. FKM (fluoroelastomer) provides strong resistance to heat and aggressive chemicals, commonly used in semiconductor fabrication. FVMQ (fluorosilicone) combines silicone’s flexibility with fluorocarbon’s chemical resistance, while VMQ (silicone rubber) is known for its high-temperature resilience and excellent sealing ability. There are also other specialized materials used for specific sealing requirements in semiconductor processing.</p></p>
