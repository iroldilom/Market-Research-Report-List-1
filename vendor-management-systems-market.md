<p><p>Vendor Management Systems (VMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise VMS solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based VMS solutions are hosted online, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as businesses pay for usage and access updates seamlessly. Both types aim to streamline vendor interactions, enhance compliance, and improve procurement efficiency.</p></p>
