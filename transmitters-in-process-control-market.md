<p><p>Transmitters in process control measure and transmit specific parameters essential for industrial operations. Pressure transmitters monitor fluid pressure levels, ensuring safety and efficiency. Temperature transmitters capture thermal data for process regulation. Flow transmitters gauge the rate of fluid movement, crucial for maintaining system balance. Level transmitters determine the amount of material in tanks or silos, preventing overflow or shortages. Vibration transmitters assess equipment health, detecting anomalies that could indicate malfunction, thereby enhancing reliability and reducing downtime in industrial processes.</p></p>
