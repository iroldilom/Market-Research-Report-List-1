<p><p>The squalane market is segmented into three main types: phyto squalane, shark squalane, and synthetic squalane. Phyto squalane, derived from plant sources like olives and sugarcane, is favored for its sustainability and skin-friendly properties. Shark squalane, obtained from shark liver oil, is traditionally popular but raises ethical concerns regarding marine life. Synthetic squalane, produced through hydrogenation of fats, offers a cruelty-free and stable alternative, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. Each type caters to different market preferences and ethical standards.</p></p>
