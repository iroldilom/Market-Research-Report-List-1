<p><ul><li>Carbon / Aramid</li><li>Aramid / Glass</li><li>Carbon / Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid fabrics market comprises three primary types: Carbon/Aramid, Aramid/Glass, and Carbon/Glass. Carbon/Aramid fabrics combine the lightweight, high-strength properties of carbon fibers with the toughness and impact resistance of aramid fibers, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Aramid/Glass fabrics blend the heat resistance of aramid with the affordability and versatility of glass fibers, often used in industrial settings. Carbon/Glass fabrics offer a balance of strength and cost-effectiveness, suitable for various applications like sports equipment and construction materials.</p></p>
