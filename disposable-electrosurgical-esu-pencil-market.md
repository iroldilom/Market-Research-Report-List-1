<p><p>The disposable electrosurgical (ESU) pencil market is segmented into two primary types: hand control and foot control. Hand control ESU pencils allow surgeons to activate the device using their fingers, providing immediate access and precise control during procedures. In contrast, foot control ESU pencils are operated by foot pedals, enabling hands-free activation, which can enhance surgical efficiency by allowing the surgeon to maintain focus on the procedure without needing to pause for manual adjustments. Both types optimize surgical performance and patient safety.</p></p>
