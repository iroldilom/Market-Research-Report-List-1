<p><p>The operating otoscope market is primarily categorized into two types: halogen operating otoscopes and xenon operating otoscopes. Halogen otoscopes utilize halogen bulbs, providing bright, consistent illumination for detailed examination of the ear canal and tympanic membrane, favored for their cost-effectiveness and reliability. Xenon otoscopes, on the other hand, use xenon lamps, delivering superior brightness and color accuracy, which enhances visualization during surgical procedures. Both types are essential in otolaryngology for accurate diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
