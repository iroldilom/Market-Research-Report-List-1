<p><ul><li>Ribs Marking</li><li>Raised Dots Marking</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tactile paving market comprises three main types: ribs marking, raised dots marking, and others. Ribs marking features ridged patterns that guide visually impaired individuals along pathways, often indicating the direction of travel. Raised dots marking consists of small, dome-like shapes that signal hazards or transitions, like road crossings. The "others" category includes alternative designs and materials tailored for specific environments or needs, enhancing accessibility and safety for individuals with visual impairments in public spaces.</p></p>
