<p><p>The fuel transfer pump market is segmented into three main types: AC fuel transfer pumps, DC fuel transfer pumps, and hand fuel transfer pumps. AC fuel transfer pumps are powered by alternating current, making them suitable for stationary applications with electric supply. DC fuel transfer pumps operate on direct current, ideal for mobile use and smaller applications. Hand fuel transfer pumps rely on manual operation, offering portability and simplicity, often used in situations where electricity is unavailable. Each type serves distinct user needs and operational environments.</p></p>
