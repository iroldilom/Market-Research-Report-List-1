<p><p>Aluminum Nitride (AlN) ceramic heaters are categorized into two main types: flat heaters and cylindrical (tube) heaters. Flat heaters feature a planar design, ideal for applications requiring uniform heating across surfaces, such as electronics and medical devices. Cylindrical heaters, shaped like tubes, are tailored for heating fluids or gases in pipes and specialized equipment. Both types leverage AlN's exceptional thermal conductivity and electrical insulation properties, making them suitable for high-performance applications in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial processes.</p></p>
