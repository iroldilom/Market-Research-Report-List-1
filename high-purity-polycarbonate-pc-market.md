<p><p>The High Purity Polycarbonate (PC) market comprises two primary production methods: the Phosgene Interface Method and the Melt Transesterification Method. The Phosgene Interface Method involves the reaction of bisphenol A with phosgene, yielding high-purity PC suitable for applications requiring superior optical and thermal properties. Alternatively, the Melt Transesterification Method transforms bisphenol A and diphenyl carbonate in a single-step reaction, offering sustainability advantages with lower environmental impact. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including electronics, medical devices, and optical lenses.</p></p>
