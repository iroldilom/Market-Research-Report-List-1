<p><p>Diffusion vacuum pumps and vapor jet pumps are essential in creating high-vacuum environments for various industrial and research applications. Oil diffusion pumps utilize a jet of vaporized oil to capture gas molecules, offering high pumping speeds and efficiency. In contrast, vapor jet pumps rely on high-speed gas streams, often using steam or other vapors, to achieve vacuum conditions without moving parts, making them suitable for sensitive processes. Both types play vital roles in semiconductor manufacturing, aerospace, and scientific research.</p></p>
