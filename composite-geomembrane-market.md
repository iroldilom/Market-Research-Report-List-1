<p><p>The composite geomembrane market includes various types such as HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and FPP (Flexible Polypropylene). HDPE is known for its strength and durability, making it suitable for landfill liners and containment. LLDPE offers flexibility and puncture resistance, while PVC is versatile and cost-effective, used in various applications. FPP combines flexibility with chemical resistance. Additionally, other materials cater to specific needs, expanding the application scope across environmental and industrial sectors.</p></p>
