<p><p>Distributed Fibre Optic Sensing (DFOS) utilizes optical fibers to measure physical parameters like temperature, strain, and pressure over long distances. The market is divided into single-mode and multi-mode fiber types. Single-mode fibers feature a smaller core, allowing for long-distance transmission of signals with minimal loss, ideal for high-precision applications. In contrast, multi-mode fibers have a larger core that supports multiple light paths, suited for shorter distances and high-capacity data transmission, making it more cost-effective for certain installations.</p></p>
