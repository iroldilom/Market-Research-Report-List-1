<p><p>Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (HNBR) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent resistance to heat, oil, and chemical exposure. The HNBR market can be segmented into three types based on the Acrylonitrile (ACN) content: low ACN (less than 18%), medium ACN (18-36%), and high ACN (more than 36%). Low ACN HNBR offers better flexibility and lower permeability, making it suitable for automotive applications. Medium ACN balances oil resistance with flexibility, while high ACN provides superior chemical resistance, ideal for industrial uses.</p></p>
