<p><p>The magnetotherapy machine market includes two primary types: magnetostatic therapy and dynamic magnetic therapy. Magnetostatic therapy utilizes static magnetic fields to promote healing and pain relief by enhancing blood circulation and reducing inflammation. In contrast, dynamic magnetic therapy involves the use of varying magnetic fields that stimulate cellular activity and metabolism. Both therapies aim to alleviate discomfort, improve tissue regeneration, and support overall wellness, catering to various health conditions and patient needs.</p></p>
