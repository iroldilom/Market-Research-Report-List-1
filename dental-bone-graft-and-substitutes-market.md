<p><p>The dental bone graft and substitutes market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Allograft** involves using bone from a human donor, which is processed and sterilized for safety.</p><p>2. **Autograft** utilizes the patient’s own bone, providing the best compatibility and integration but requires an additional surgical site.</p><p>3. **Xenograft** employs bone from non-human animals, usually processed to reduce immunogenicity.</p><p>4. **Synthetic grafts** are man-made materials, often composed of biocompatible substances, promoting bone regeneration without biological tissue. Each type offers unique benefits for dental procedures.</p></p>
