<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra-high resolution camera market comprises two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture entire images simultaneously, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed snapshots, such as industrial inspections and medical imaging. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suited for continuous processes like scanning materials on a conveyor belt. Both types deliver high-resolution imaging, but their operational mechanics cater to distinct application needs across various industries.</p></p>
