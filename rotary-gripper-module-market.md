<p><ul><li>Parallel</li><li>Angular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Gripper Module market can be divided into two primary types: parallel and angular grippers. Parallel grippers maintain a consistent distance between their jaws, making them ideal for handling flat objects with precision. Angular grippers, on the other hand, open and close at an angle, allowing for better access to items in tight spaces and accommodating irregular shapes. Both types enhance automation in various industries by improving efficiency in material handling and assembly processes.</p></p>
