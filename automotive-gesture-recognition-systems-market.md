<p><p>Automotive gesture recognition systems are categorized into two main types: touch-based and touchless systems. Touch-based systems require physical interaction, where users must tap or swipe surfaces to control vehicle functions, providing tactile feedback. In contrast, touchless systems utilize cameras and sensors to detect hand movements and gestures without physical contact, enhancing convenience and safety. Both systems aim to improve user experience and minimize distractions, allowing drivers to interact seamlessly with infotainment and control systems while focusing on the road.</p></p>
