<p><p>The lead-acid battery market comprises three main types: VRLA batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA (Valved Regulated Lead Acid) batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and designed for applications requiring dependable energy storage. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, feature liquid electrolyte and necessitate periodic maintenance, making them suitable for industrial uses. The "others" category includes niche variants like inherently safe lead-acid technologies and specialty designs for specific applications. Together, these types address diverse energy storage needs across various sectors.</p></p>
