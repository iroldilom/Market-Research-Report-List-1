<p><p>The Underwear Care Machines market features three primary types: Ultraviolet Sterilization, Vacuum Sterilization, and High-Temperature Sterilization. Ultraviolet Sterilization employs UV light to eliminate bacteria and pathogens, ensuring hygiene without chemicals. Vacuum Sterilization uses low pressure to prevent moisture, effectively removing contaminants while preserving fabric integrity. High-Temperature Sterilization involves using steam or high heat to kill germs, offering a deep clean. Each method addresses unique consumer needs for sanitation, convenience, and garment care, enhancing overall hygiene practices in personal apparel.</p></p>
