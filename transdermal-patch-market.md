<p><p>A transdermal patch is a medical device applied to the skin that delivers medication through the dermal layers into the bloodstream. The applications of transdermal patches span various therapeutic areas, including pain management, hormonal therapies, and the delivery of vaccines.</p><p>The Transdermal Patch Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases and a rising demand for painless drug delivery systems. Innovations in patch technology, such as microneedle and programmable release mechanisms, are enhancing the efficacy and comfort of transdermal delivery. The incorporation of smart technologies, such as wearable patches with monitoring capabilities, is another emerging trend that is reshaping the market landscape.
