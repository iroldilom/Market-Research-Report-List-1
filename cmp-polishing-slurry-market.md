<p><p>CMP polishing slurry is essential in semiconductor and electronics manufacturing for achieving smooth surfaces on wafers. Alumina slurry, known for its high removal rate and excellent planarization, is widely used for initial polishing stages. Colloidal silica slurry offers superior surface finish and is ideal for final polishing applications due to its fine particles. Ceria slurry, containing cerium oxide, is effective for hard substrates and delivers high precision polishing. Each type caters to specific processing needs, enhancing overall production efficiency.</p></p>
