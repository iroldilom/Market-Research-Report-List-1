<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Chlorinator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Pool Chlorinator</li><li>Offline Pool Chlorinator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool chlorinator market is primarily divided into two types: inline and offline chlorinators. Inline pool chlorinators are integrated directly into the filtration system, allowing for continuous and automatic chlorine adjustment during water circulation, which ensures consistent sanitation. In contrast, offline pool chlorinators operate independently from the filtration system, dispensing chlorine when needed but requiring manual monitoring and maintenance. Both types offer convenience and efficiency in maintaining optimal water quality in swimming pools.</p></p>
