<p><strong>The Chemical Sticky Anchor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical sticky anchor market primarily comprises injectable adhesive anchors and capsule adhesive anchors. Injectable adhesive anchors are liquefied adhesives that can be injected into drilled holes, providing flexibility in installation and allowing for precise application. Capsule adhesive anchors, on the other hand, come in pre-packaged capsules that mix the adhesive components upon application. Both types offer robust bonding solutions for construction and renovation projects, ensuring strong anchors for loads in various materials, including concrete and masonry.</p></p>
