<p><p>The masking tape for floors market is segmented into two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided masking tape features adhesive on one side and is primarily used for temporarily marking or protecting surfaces, ensuring clean lines during painting or remodeling. In contrast, double-sided masking tape has adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for more versatile applications such as securing rugs, carpets, or floor coverings without visible tape edges. Both types offer distinct functionalities tailored to various flooring needs.</p></p>
