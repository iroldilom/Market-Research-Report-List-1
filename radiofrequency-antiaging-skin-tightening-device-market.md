<p><p>The radiofrequency anti-aging skin-tightening device market is primarily divided into bipolar and monopolar technology types. Bipolar technology uses two electrodes placed close together, delivering targeted energy to the treatment area, resulting in effective tissue heating and skin tightening. This method offers precise control and targeted results. In contrast, monopolar technology employs a single electrode with a return pad placed at a distance, allowing energy to penetrate deeper into the skin layers, promoting collagen production and more substantial tightening effects.</p></p>
