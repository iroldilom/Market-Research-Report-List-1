<p><p>The desiccant wheel market is categorized into three main types: Silica Gel Desiccant Wheel, Molecular Sieve Desiccant Wheel, and Others. Silica Gel Wheels utilize silica gel's high moisture-absorption capacity for efficient drying processes, commonly used in HVAC systems. Molecular Sieve Wheels leverage the advanced adsorption properties of crystalline aluminosilicates to target specific moisture levels, ideal for critical applications.
