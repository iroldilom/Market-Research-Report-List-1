<p><p>The thin film ceramic substrates market includes various types, each with unique properties. Alumina (Al2O3) is widely used for its excellent electrical insulation and thermal conductivity. Aluminium Nitride (AlN) offers superior thermal properties and is ideal for high-power applications. Beryllium Oxide (BeO) is known for its high thermal conductivity and is often used in advanced electronic devices but requires handling precautions. Silicon Nitride (Si3N4) is recognized for its mechanical strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding environments.</p></p>
