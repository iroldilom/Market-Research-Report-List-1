<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler(BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use(POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking fountains (water dispensers) market primarily consists of two types: Bottled Water Dispensers (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWC systems utilize large water bottles that are manually replaced, providing a portable and flexible solution, ideal for homes or small offices. In contrast, POU systems are plumbed directly into the water supply, offering a continuous flow of filtered water, making them suitable for larger facilities and commercial settings. Each type addresses different user needs and preferences for hydration solutions.</p></p>
